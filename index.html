Why Software Testing?

Why did I choose to study software testing?
Like I said ,I'm a teacher and over time I have found that it is not really challenging or interesting to me. So I decided to study something else. For a long time I was debating which profession could suit me and after searches and consultations I discovered the software testing. I researched a bit about software testing and realized that it requires systemic and creative thinking, order and organization, and looking at the small details. In addition, I realized that software testing is very important for ensuring the quality of high-tech products. During the course I discovered that being a software tester is not only finding where there are software glitches but also thinking creatively how I as a software tester can make a product better and always take care to improve it. This, in order to make the product easier and more convenient to use.
